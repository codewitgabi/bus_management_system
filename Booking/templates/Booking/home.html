How to Book

I. Click on the Reserve a seat today Button

II. You will be redirected to the booking page where you shall provide all necessary information

III. Once the form has been completely filled, Proceed for payment.

IV. Any booking that is not paid for after 24hrs will be rendered null and inactive.

V. Ensure you get a confirmation after payment otherwise make a complaint.
